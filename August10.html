WHETSTONE RADIO COLLECTIVE
Niki Nakazawa discusses her podcast “Mezcal Hour,” which explores the history, culture, and production of mezcal in Oaxaca, Mexico. Each episode will reveal more about mezcal from a historical standpoint, and explore cooperatives, prohibition, and migration—along with terminology and tasting notes.

“In the world there are approximately 220 species of agave. In Mexico, about 40 species are used in the production of mezcal and the majority of the biodiversity of mezcal is located in Oaxaca, which is why Oaxaca is generally the starting point when talking about mezcal.”
- Niki Nakazawa
