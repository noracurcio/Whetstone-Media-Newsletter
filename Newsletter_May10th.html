WELCOME TO OUR WEEKLY NEWSLETTER

Since 2016 Whetstone has used food as a means of better understanding humans and the world. To date we’ve worked with a team of global creatives representing over 80 countries. We believe that diversity isn’t just noteworthy, it’s what makes our work so essential. When the gatekeepers are diverse, so too are the stories, its tellers and their experiences. This diversity accelerates our collective knowledge and empathy. Whetstone is unequivocally and gratefully a better company because of it.



HIGH ON THE HOG:

Whetstone's founder, Stephen Satterfield will be hosting Netflix's new docs-series, High on the Hog. The series is based on one of food historian Dr. Jessica B. Harris' books. According to the press release the show will be "part culinary show, part travelogue" and is a "story of Black America’s resilience, enduring creativity, and vital contribution to America’s kitchen."

IMPACT AWARDS

In addition to this recognition we are the grateful recipients of $10,000 that will be put to use further our collectively personal stories of food origin, ancestry and global gastronomy.
