Welcome to our weekly newsletter

Since 2016, Whetstone has used food as a means to better understand humans and the world. To date, we’ve worked with a team of global creatives representing over 80 countries. We believe that diversity isn’t just noteworthy, it’s what makes our work so essential. When the storytellers are diverse, so too are their experiences. This diversity accelerates our collective knowledge and empathy. Whetstone is unequivocally and gratefully a better company because of it.

High on the hog:

Whetstone's founder, Stephen Satterfield, will be hosting Netflix's new docuseries, High On The Hog. The series is based on a book by food historian, Dr. Jessica B. Harris. The show will be "part culinary show, part travelogue" and is a "story of Black America’s resilience, enduring creativity, and vital contribution to America’s kitchen" according to the press release. The air date is May 26.

IMPACT AWARDS

We are honored to announce that Whetstone, along with the makers of the extraordinary film, Gather, have been announced as the 2021 Impact Media Award Winners. In addition to this recognition, we are the grateful recipients of $10,000 that will be used to further our collective personal stories of food origin, ancestry and global gastronomy.
